常見問題 FAQ

教練，我應該選擇一對一還是小組訓練？ Coach, should I choose 1-on-1 or group training?
這取決於您的個人目標。如果您需要高度精緻的動作糾正、個人化飲食指導，一對一私人訓練效果最顯著。如果您希望與朋友共同進步並降低預算，小組訓練則是理想選擇。
It depends on your goals. For precise form correction and personalized nutrition, 1-on-1 is most effective. If you want to progress with friends on a budget, group training is the ideal choice.
我有舊患或長期痛症（如腰痛、膝頭痛），仲操唔操得？ I have old injuries or chronic pain, can I still train?
絕對可以。我具備糾正性訓練專業背景，會先評估你的活動度，避開受傷風險，並透過加強弱勢肌肉來減輕痛症。
Absolutely. With my corrective exercise background, I assess your mobility to ensure safety and strengthen key muscles to alleviate pain.
訓練地點在哪裡？ Where is the training location?
我主要在 Markham 及 Richmond Hill 進行授課。環境專業且具備高隱私度，能讓你專注於訓練。
I primarily teach in Markham and Richmond Hill. The environments are professional and private, allowing you to focus on your training.
我需要跟隨非常嚴格的飲食單 (Meal Plan) 嗎？ Do I need to follow a very strict meal plan?
不需要。我主張「可持續性」的飲食法。我會教你如何計算宏觀營養素 (Macros)，讓你學會在享受喜歡的食物時，仍能達到目標。
No. I advocate for a "sustainable" approach. I will teach you how to track macros, allowing you to enjoy the foods you love while still reaching your goals.
我完全沒有健身經驗，會不會跟不上進度？ I have zero gym experience. Will it be too difficult?
完全不會。我會根據你的體能水平「由零開始」編排課程，重點會先放在建立正確動作模式，確保你在安全情況下進步。
Not at all. I design programs from scratch based on your level, focusing on proper movement patterns to ensure you progress safely.
