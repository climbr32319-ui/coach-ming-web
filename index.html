FAQ / 常見問題

上課地點在哪裡？ / Where are the sessions held? +
主要於多倫多地區的健身房進行，特別是 Markham 及 Richmond Hill 區域。具體地點將於諮詢時確認。
Mainly held in Toronto area gyms (Markham/Richmond Hill). Exact locations will be confirmed during consultation.
新手適合參加訓練嗎？ / Are the sessions suitable for beginners? +
非常適合。我會從基礎的呼吸模式及核心穩定性開始教學，確保動作安全且有效。
Absolutely. I start with fundamental breathing and core stability to ensure all movements are safe and effective.
收費模式是如何計算的？ / What is the pricing model? +
我們提供靈活的訓練課堂組合方案。詳細報價將在完成初步評估並了解您的目標後提供。
We offer flexible training packages. Detailed quotes will be provided after an initial assessment.
如果臨時有事需要更改時間嗎？ / What is the cancellation policy? +
請於課堂開始前至少 24 小時通知，以便安排改期或補課。
Please provide at least 24 hours' notice for any rescheduling or cancellations.
